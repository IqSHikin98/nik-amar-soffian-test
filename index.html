About Us

Welcome to Nik Amar Soffian, a dynamic and client-focused legal practice committed to delivering dependable and effective legal services throughout Malaysia.

The firm was established in April 2024 and has since evolved through strategic collaboration and partnership expansion in August 2024, strengthening its professional capacity and broadening its areas of practice.

At Nik Amar Soffian, we believe in building strong and lasting relationships with our clients. We take the time to understand each client’s unique circumstances and objectives, allowing us to provide practical, strategic, and solution-oriented legal advice.

Our areas of practice include Civil Litigation, Corporate & Commercial Law, Family Law, and related advisory services. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of integrity, professionalism, and ethical legal practice in every matter entrusted to us.

Whether representing individuals, small and medium enterprises, or corporate entities, our focus remains on delivering clear guidance and responsible legal representation.

We welcome the opportunity to assist you with your legal matters.
