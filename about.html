Nik Amar Soffian

Advocates & Solicitors

Nik Amar Soffian is a boutique law firm dedicated to delivering strategic, responsive and solution-oriented legal services across Malaysia.

Founded with a vision to provide high-quality legal representation grounded in professionalism and integrity, the firm serves individuals, entrepreneurs, small and medium enterprises, and corporate entities. We believe that effective legal representation goes beyond technical knowledge — it requires clear communication, commercial awareness and an unwavering commitment to our clients’ objectives.

Our practice encompasses civil litigation, corporate advisory, property transactions, family law and criminal defence matters. We regularly advise clients on complex disputes, contractual issues, compliance matters and strategic legal planning. Whether representing clients in negotiations or court proceedings, we remain focused on achieving practical and sustainable outcomes.

At Nik Amar Soffian, we understand that legal matters can significantly impact businesses and personal lives. For this reason, we approach every engagement with diligence, discretion and thorough preparation. Our firm is committed to delivering tailored advice that reflects the unique circumstances of each client.

As we continue to grow, our commitment remains unchanged: to uphold the highest standards of ethical practice while providing dependable and strategic legal solutions that clients can trust.
