Aniq Tauhid specialises in civil litigation, corporate advisory, and commercial dispute resolution. He represents clients across various levels of court and regularly advises small and medium enterprises as well as corporations on contractual and compliance matters.

Throughout his practice, Aniq has handled a wide range of disputes, including contractual disagreements, debt recovery matters, property conflicts and commercial litigation cases. He is also actively involved in advising businesses on corporate structuring and regulatory compliance.

In the area of property and conveyancing, Aniq has assisted clients in residential and commercial transactions, ensuring that each matter is completed efficiently while safeguarding his clients’ legal and financial interests.

Known for his strategic thinking and practical problem-solving approach, Aniq prioritises achieving favourable outcomes through negotiation where appropriate, while remaining prepared to advocate firmly for his clients in court.

With a strong commitment to integrity, diligence and professionalism, Aniq strives to deliver clear guidance and effective representation tailored to each client’s needs.
