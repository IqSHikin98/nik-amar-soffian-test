Soffian Azmir is the Principal Lawyer of Nik Amar Soffian, bringing with him extensive experience in civil litigation, corporate matters and property transactions. He is committed to delivering strategic, practical and client-focused legal solutions tailored to each individual and business.

Throughout his years in practice, Soffian has represented clients in a wide range of disputes involving contractual disagreements, debt recovery, property conflicts and commercial litigation matters. He has also advised corporations and small to medium enterprises on regulatory compliance, corporate structuring and risk management.

In the area of property and conveyancing, Soffian has handled numerous residential and commercial transactions, ensuring smooth completion while safeguarding his clients’ interests. His meticulous approach ensures clarity, transparency and confidence throughout every transaction.

Beyond litigation and advisory work, Soffian is known for his strong negotiation skills and pragmatic problem-solving abilities. He prioritises early resolution where appropriate, while remaining prepared to vigorously advocate for his clients in court when necessary.

With a firm belief that trust is the foundation of effective legal representation, Soffian continues to uphold the highest standards of integrity, professionalism and diligence in every matter entrusted to him.
